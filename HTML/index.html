What is Jola?

Jola is your new voice assistant designed to really simplify your life. This cute magnetic device is made to stick on your fridge and to provide you the total control of it.

Features

Shopping list assistant

Your son has finished the milk without telling you once again? Jola keeps you informed of what you have or don't have in your fridge. Just ask it. Save time when making your shopping list.

Food waste fighter

Jola fights with you against food waste. Your assistant is here to warn you if something is approaching the expiry date.

Recipe generator

No more ideas for the dinner? Ask Jola to give you yummy recipes with what you have in your fridge.

Plastic wrap tracker

Did you know that an average EU inhabitant generates 174 kg of packaging waste per year? Jola allows you to reduce drastically this number by tracking the plastic wrap going in and out of your fridge and giving useful recommandations accordingly.

How it works?

Jola voice assistant is composed of two parts that are connected to each other. The one that you stick on your fridge which hears and speaks with you. And the other one that you place in your fridge. It is this last one that collects all the precious information. Thanks to its powerful detector it scans the nature of an item, the expiry date, the volume and the weight of it.
You can configure Jola as you wish. You can choose to have its reminders at a specific time of the day, to have it only when opening your fridge or even only when asking it.

Our brand

Jola is a French brand based in Paris. Founded in 2017, the project was born from the observation that, although our awareness of environmental issues rises, our behaviours are still not enought. One of the reasons? Our lack of time. We know that it takes time to find out the good alternatives to adopt, to track the expiry dates of all the items that you have in the fridge, to be attentive on the quantity of plastic you consume...
That is why Jola came to life. With Jola you have the good recommandations, at the the good time and based on your own consumption. All the more, Jola makes you explore new things by generating recipes that can get you out of the routine.

No more excuses to do its share in building a greener future. Start it today!
